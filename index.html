Getting Started

Welcome to the DaisyDriver website! DaisyDriver is a motor control system designed for integrating actuators within a rapid prototyping design process. It was designed at the University of Cambridge. It has been used for experiments here in the physics department, and has been used in space. A publication describing many aspects of the device can be found here TBA.

This page should help you with any questions you might have when you're getting started.
